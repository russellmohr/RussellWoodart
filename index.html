Welcome to Russell's Woodart

Welcome to Russell’s Woodart, a space where nature becomes art and art becomes a moment of peace. I work with wood in many forms — from the swirling grain of burl to pieces touched with epoxy — always seeking to honor the wonder that already exists in the world around us. Each creation is a meditation on balance, beauty, and the deep connection between nature and creativity.
